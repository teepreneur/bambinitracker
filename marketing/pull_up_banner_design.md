# Bambini Tracker Pull-Up Banner Design 🏳️

**Dimensions**: 850mm x 2000mm (Standard Pull-Up)  
**Goal**: High-impact brand awareness at expos, parenting clinics, and community events.

---

## 🔝 Section 1: Header (300mm)
- **Background**: Soft Coral Gradient (`#FF8C7A` to `#FFA792`)
- **Logo**: Oversized white version of the Bambini icon and wordmark centered.
- **Tagline**: *Empowering your child's developmental journey.* (White, bold, centered).

---

## 🎨 Section 2: Hero Shot (1000mm - Eye Level)
- **Visual**: A large, high-definition illustration (generated by AI) of a smiling Black father and mother playing with their toddler on a soft, minimalist play mat.
- **Overlay**: Subtle glassmorphism card floating on the right side with a screenshot of the "Activity Detail" screen from the app.

---

## 📝 Section 3: Core Messaging (400mm)
**Headline**: "Tracking Milestones, Creating Memories." (Dark Teal, `#1A5F5B`)

**Bullet Points** (with custom icons):
- ✅ **Scientifically Backed**: CDC-aligned tracking for ages 0-6.
- 🤖 **AI-Personalized**: Activities tailored to your child's unique pace.
- 🧸 **Physical Play**: Interactive toys and kits delivered to your door.

---

## 🤳 Section 4: Call to Action (300mm)
- **Visual**: A large, easy-to-scan QR code in a white box.
- **Text**: "Scan to start your journey."
- **Badges**: App Store and Google Play "Get it now" badges side-by-side.
- **Footer**: Website URL: `bambinitracker.app` | Branding hashtag: `#BambiniHero`

- **Primary**: Coral (`#FF8C7A`)
